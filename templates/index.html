Create Your First Experiment

How Habit-Lab Works

1. Define Your Question

Start with a simple question like: “Does morning study or evening study work better for me?”

2. Add Options A & B

Create two strategies you want to compare. Keep it simple and test one variable at a time.

3. Set Your Metric

Choose how you’ll measure success — focus, mood, productivity, time spent, etc.

4. Log Daily Results

Each day, record which option you used and how well it performed.

5. Compare Results

View charts, averages, and insights to see which option performs better.

6. Build Better Habits

Use your findings to choose the best strategy and improve your habits long‑term.
