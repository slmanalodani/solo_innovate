About HabitLab

Your personal A/B testing lab for habits, routines, and lifestyle experiments.

What is HabitLab?

HabitLab helps you run simple A/B tests on your daily life. Instead of guessing which habit works better, you test two options and track your results over a set number of days.

How It Works

  • Create an experiment with two options (A and B).
  • Choose a metric you want to measure (focus, mood, energy, etc.).
  • Log your daily results.
  • HabitLab analyzes your data and shows which option performs better.

Why HabitLab?

Most people rely on intuition to improve their habits. HabitLab replaces guesswork with simple data. It’s lightweight, fast, and built to help you make better decisions about your routines.
